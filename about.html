The Park Center Automotive Tradition

“I wanted to try to open a place and just be honest with people”

Park Center Automotive was established in July 1992. We are conveniently located next to the Manassas Park City Hall and VRE. Frequent visitors to the shop always discover something new. Park Center Automotive boasts an impressive collection of antique engines, hot rods, gadgets, and side projects. There's always something going on here!

"I love doing this, I can’t wait to get up in the morning"

Park Center Automotive was recently recognized by the Manassas Park City Council for twenty oustanding years in business. The small council meeting was flooded with supporters wearing Park Center Automotive shirts, eager to voice their support. David received his accolades to a standing ovation.

“I appreciate the people who have kept me in business this long. I want to give them a different experience; it’s the trust"

We generate all of our advertising via word of mouth. We know that the quality of our work speaks for itself, and we are confident that our customers will recommend us. This business was built from the ground up with a focus on quality, commitment, and integrity. We strive to go above and beyond for our customers!

David Ford (left), owner of Park Center Automotive, and his family. Thanksgiving 2017.
