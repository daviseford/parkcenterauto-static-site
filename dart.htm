1972 Dodge dart HEMI

Featured in MOPAR ACTION Magazine


400 HP, a kickin' sound system, and no worries


I built this car by repurposing a 5.7 HEMI drive train from a 2004 Dodge Ram Hemi 1500 2 wheel drive truck. I used all of the factory harnesses, computers, fuel pump, etc. to bring its reliability and driveabilty into this century.

I also upgraded the suspension and stiffened the unit body. Four wheel Wilwood four-piston caliper disc brakes stop the car with little effort. Coddington rims and BFG rubber. It handles very well.


rock solid dependable


This Dart gets 22 mpg with the 5 speed automatic and 3:91 gears, and after 10,000 cruising miles it has been rock solid dependable. It has had only oil changes since completion. That's never happened in my universe before. 400 HP, a kickin' sound system, and no worries makes one want to drive this car all of the time.


It really is as cool as it looks


The satisfaction that I have gained from the Dart has led me to begin anew; I started on a '68 Barracuda recently - and it's going to get the SRT-8 driveline. Stay tuned for that one. I can't wait.

- David Ford
